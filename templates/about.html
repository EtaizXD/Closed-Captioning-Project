ABOUT US

This web application was created as part of the research project entitled Effectiveness of the Use of Conversation Analysis (CA)-Informed Closed Captions in Improving Spoken English of Thai EFL Learners, evolving from the initiative to improve English conversation skills of Thai EFL learners via the CA-informed Teaching (CA-T) approach. This collaborative project, funded by the grant # LIA6701014S from the National Science, Research and Innovation Fund (NSRF) and Prince of Songkla University, involves the Research Center for Language, Culture, and Human Development in Lower ASEAN at the Faculty of Liberal Arts, Prince of Songkla University (PSU) and the Faculty of Informatics, Burapha University. Its objective is to enhance English pronunciation and proficiency among Thai EFL learners by integrating CA-informed closed captions into English conversation learning materials.
